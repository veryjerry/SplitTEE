4. Payment Terms

4.1 Fees and Charges

Split Tee charges a convenience fee of 3% of each Player's portion, with a minimum of $2.00 and a maximum of $5.00 per transaction. This fee is charged to each Player at the time of payment.

4.2 Payment Processing

All payments are processed through Stripe, Inc. By using the Service, you agree to Stripe's Terms of Service. We do not store your full payment card information on our servers.

4.3 Payment Window

Important: Each Split has a limited Payment Window (typically 10 minutes after confirmation). If all Players do not complete payment within the Payment Window, the Split will expire and all completed payments will be automatically refunded.

4.4 Authorization Holds

For tee times within 6 days of the booking, we may place an authorization hold on your payment method rather than an immediate charge. The hold will be captured at the tee time or released if the Split expires or is cancelled.

5. Refunds and Cancellations

5.1 Automatic Refunds

Refunds are automatically processed when:

  • The Payment Window expires before all Players have paid
  • The Booker cancels the Split before completion
  • A Course Partner cancels the associated tee time

5.2 Refund Processing Time

Refunds are initiated immediately but may take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement, depending on your financial institution.

5.3 Convenience Fee Refunds

Convenience fees are refunded in full when refunds are issued due to Split expiration or cancellation by the Course Partner. Fees are not refunded for voluntary cancellations after Split completion.
