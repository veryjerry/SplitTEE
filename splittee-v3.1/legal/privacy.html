Dorn Ventures LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") operates the Split Tee payment splitting platform (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our Service.

Summary: We collect only what's necessary to process payments and improve the Service. We never sell your personal data. Your payment information is handled securely by Stripe.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide the Service: Process payments, send notifications, manage splits
  • Communicate with You: Payment confirmations, reminders, support responses
  • Improve the Service: Analytics, feature development, bug fixes
  • Ensure Security: Fraud prevention, authentication, abuse detection
  • Comply with Law: Legal obligations, dispute resolution, enforcing terms

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only in these circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing (see Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • SendGrid/Twilio: Email and SMS notifications
  • Cloud Hosting: Data storage and processing

3.2 Course Partners

We share with Course Partners only the information necessary to confirm bookings:

  • Names of players in the group
  • Payment confirmation status
  • Booking reference details

We do not share your payment card details with Course Partners.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights, safety, or property.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the successor entity.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Payment card data handled exclusively by PCI-compliant Stripe
  • Secure password hashing (bcrypt)
  • Rate limiting and abuse prevention
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Employee access controls and training

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but strive to protect your data using reasonable safeguards.
