Course Partner Agreement

Terms and conditions for golf course integration with Split Tee

This Course Partner Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between Dorn Ventures LLC, a Michigan limited liability company doing business as Split Tee ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"), and the golf course or facility identified during registration ("Partner," "you," or "your").

By registering for a Split Tee partner account or integrating the Split Tee platform, you agree to be bound by this Agreement.

Partnership Summary: You integrate Split Tee on your booking site. Golfers split payments among their group. You receive 100% of your tee time price. We collect a 3% convenience fee ($2-$5) from each player.

1. Definitions

  • "Platform" means the Split Tee payment splitting service, including APIs, widgets, dashboards, and related technology.
  • "Integration" means the embedding of Split Tee functionality on Partner's website or booking system.
  • "Split" means a payment arrangement where multiple golfers share the cost of a tee time.
  • "Booker" means the individual who initiates a Split.
  • "Player" means any individual participating in a Split, including the Booker.
  • "Tee Time Price" means the total cost of the tee time as set by Partner.
  • "Convenience Fee" means the fee charged by Company to Players for using the Platform.

2. Partner Registration

2.1 Eligibility

To become a Partner, you must:

  • Operate a legitimate golf course, driving range, or golf facility
  • Have authority to enter into binding agreements on behalf of your organization
  • Maintain a valid Stripe Connect account
  • Comply with all applicable laws and regulations

2.2 Registration Process

  1. Complete the online registration form with accurate information
  2. Connect your Stripe account via Stripe Connect OAuth
  3. Verify your email address and business information
  4. Receive API credentials upon approval

2.3 Account Security

You are responsible for:

  • Maintaining the confidentiality of your API keys and credentials
  • All activity that occurs under your account
  • Immediately notifying us of any unauthorized access
  • Implementing reasonable security measures for your integration

3. Integration Requirements

3.1 Technical Integration

You may integrate the Platform using:

  • Simple Embed: Add our JavaScript widget to your booking page
  • API Mode: Call our API from your backend to create payment links

3.2 Required Information

For each Split, you must provide:

  • Accurate tee time date and time
  • Total price for the tee time (must match what you would charge directly)
  • Number of players in the group
  • Course name and any relevant booking reference
Price Integrity: You must not inflate prices for Split Tee bookings compared to direct bookings. Violations may result in immediate termination.

3.3 Webhook Configuration

You should configure webhooks to receive real-time notifications about:

  • Split creation and timer start
  • Individual player payments
  • Split completion or expiration
  • Refund events

4. Payment Terms

4.1 Fee Structure

Fee Type Amount Paid By
Convenience Fee 3% of player's portion ($2 min, $5 max) Each Player
Platform Fee to Partner $0 N/A
Stripe Processing Standard Stripe rates Partner (from their portion)

4.2 Payment Flow

  1. Players pay their portion plus convenience fee via Stripe
  2. Convenience fees are retained by Company
  3. Tee time amounts are transferred to your Stripe Connect account
  4. Stripe's standard payout schedule applies to your account

4.3 Payment Modes

  • Authorization Hold (≤6 days to tee time): Funds are held and captured at tee time
  • Immediate Capture (>6 days to tee time): Funds are captured immediately

5. Partner Obligations

5.1 Booking Fulfillment

You agree to:

  • Honor all confirmed and fully-paid Splits at the stated price
  • Provide the same service quality as direct bookings
  • Not require additional payment from Players beyond the Split amount
  • Maintain accurate availability information

5.2 Customer Service

You are responsible for:

  • All course-related customer service (check-in, facility issues, weather policies)
  • Your own cancellation and refund policies for tee times
  • Communicating your policies clearly to golfers

We handle all payment-related customer service for the Split Tee platform.

5.3 Compliance

7. Refunds and Cancellations

7.1 Automatic Refunds

The Platform automatically refunds Players when:

  • The payment timer expires before all Players pay
  • The Booker cancels before Split completion
  • You cancel or cannot fulfill the tee time

7.2 Partner-Initiated Refunds

You may request refunds for Players through your dashboard when:

  • The tee time is cancelled due to weather or course conditions
  • You choose to honor a cancellation request per your policies
  • A booking error occurred

7.3 Refund Responsibility

For refunds you initiate after Split completion:

  • The full tee time amount is refunded to Players
  • The convenience fee is NOT refunded (retained by Company)
  • You bear any Stripe fees associated with the refund

8. Data and Privacy

8.1 Data Sharing

We share with you:

  • Player names for the booking
  • Payment confirmation status
  • Booking reference information

We do NOT share with you:

  • Player payment card details
  • Player financial information
  • Data from other Partners

8.2 Data Use

You may use Player data only for:

  • Communicating about the booking

You may NOT use Player data for marketing without separate consent from Players.
