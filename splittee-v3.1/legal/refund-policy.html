Refund Policy

Clear and fair refund guidelines for Split Tee payments

Last Updated: [DATE] | Effective Date: [DATE]
Quick Summary: If your payment split doesn't complete (timer expires, booking cancelled), you get a full automatic refund including the convenience fee. Completed bookings follow the golf course's cancellation policy.

1. How Split Tee Payments Work

Understanding our payment flow helps clarify when refunds apply:

  1. A Booker creates a payment split for a tee time
  2. Players receive payment links via email/SMS
  3. A countdown timer starts (typically 10 minutes)
  4. All players must complete payment before the timer expires
  5. If successful, the booking is confirmed with the golf course
  6. If the timer expires, all payments are automatically refunded

2. Automatic Refund Scenarios

In these cases, refunds are processed automatically with no action required from you:

✓ Timer Expiration

If not all players complete payment before the countdown timer expires, everyone who paid receives a full refund including the convenience fee.

Refund amount: 100% of payment + 100% of convenience fee

Processing time: Initiated immediately

✓ Booker Cancellation (Before Completion)

If the Booker cancels the split before all players have paid, everyone who has already paid receives a full refund.

Refund amount: 100% of payment + 100% of convenience fee

Processing time: Initiated immediately

✓ Course Partner Cancellation

If the golf course cancels or cannot honor the tee time, all players receive full refunds.

Refund amount: 100% of payment + 100% of convenience fee

Processing time: Within 24 hours of course notification

✓ Authorization Hold Release

For tee times within 6 days, we may place authorization holds instead of immediate charges. If the split expires or is cancelled, the hold is released (not a refund—the charge never occurred).

Hold release time: Typically immediate, but may take 1-7 business days to reflect depending on your bank

3. Post-Completion Cancellations

Important: Once a split is fully paid and confirmed, the golf course's cancellation policy applies—not Split Tee's. We facilitate the payment; the course controls the booking.

! Cancellation After Completion

If you need to cancel after a split is completed:

  • Contact the golf course directly about their cancellation policy
  • If the course approves a refund, they will process it through Split Tee
  • Convenience fees are NOT refunded for voluntary cancellations after completion

4. Non-Refundable Situations

✕ When Refunds Do Not Apply

  • Completed bookings where you simply change your mind
  • No-shows at the golf course
  • Weather conditions (unless course policy provides for weather cancellations)
  • Disputes between group members about who should pay
  • Convenience fees after voluntary post-completion cancellation

5. Convenience Fee Refund Policy

Scenario Convenience Fee Refunded?

Booker cancels before completion ✓ Yes, fully refunded

Course approves post-completion refund ✕ No (tee time amount only)

7. Dispute Resolution

7.1 If You Don't See Your Refund

  1. Wait 10 business days from the refund initiation date
  2. Check your email for refund confirmation from Split Tee
  3. Contact your bank to inquire about pending credits
  4. If still unresolved, contact us at [SUPPORT EMAIL] with your payment details

7.2 Disputing a Charge

Before filing a dispute with your bank:

  1. Contact us at [SUPPORT EMAIL]—we can often resolve issues faster than the dispute process
  2. Provide your payment confirmation email and describe the issue
  3. We respond to all inquiries within 2 business days

Filing a chargeback without contacting us first may delay resolution and could affect your ability to use the Service.

8. Special Circumstances

8.1 Partial Group Changes

If one player in a completed group needs to drop out:

  • The booking remains valid for remaining players
  • Individual refunds are at the golf course's discretion
  • Contact the course directly to discuss options

8.2 Technical Failures

If a payment fails due to a Split Tee technical issue:

  • Any charged amounts will be automatically refunded
  • We will work with you to rebook if desired
  • Contact support immediately if you experience issues
